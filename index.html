About Me

Hi there! My name is William Kong and I'm a postdoctoral researcher at Oak Ridge National Labs. My current interests in mathematics include computational mathematics, optimization theory, and statistics. Other than mathematics, I have interests in algorithms, functional programming, and Hapkido.

My first degree is a B.Math in Mathematical Finance from the University of Waterloo, my second is a M.Sc in Computational Science & Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology, and my most recent is a Ph.D in Operations Research also from the Georgia Institute of Technology.
